Cavalier King Charles Spaniel

The Cavalier King Charles spaniel is my favorite breed of dog. I had one named Cammie for 14 years!

The cavalier can be one of four colors:

  1. Black & tan
  2. Tricolor (black, tan & white)
  3. Blenheim (ruby & white)
  4. Ruby
